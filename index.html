Project Description :

“Battle For Supremacy” is a 2D fighting game, with local multiplayer up to 2 players in which two divinities fight to dominate the map.

The game takes place in an evolutive map, with an innovative concept of “common” health bar named Domination bar. When a player hits his opponent, the Domination bar increases, when he takes a hit, it decreases. The first one to achieve 100% domination wins the fight.

When the Domination bar of a player increases, the map evolve to support the side of the player who has the advantage.
